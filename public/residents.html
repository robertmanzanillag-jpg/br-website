Resident — 001

Zamurai

Zamurai is a 20-year-old Argentine DJ and producer who has crafted a sound as precise and versatile as it is visceral. She channels raw emotion through hypnotic rhythms and deep grooves that come alive on the dance floor. Over the past few years she has left her mark on clubs, basements, and warehouses across Argentina and the United States.

With Latin roots and a lifelong passion for movement and music, her sets balance tension and release — rooted in techno, always flirting with trance, tribal sounds, and bass-heavy textures.

Creator of Digital Funk, a radio/podcast space that celebrates musical expansion and real connection between artists from around the world.

Resident — 002

PROLETAR

23 year old Romanian-born, Miami-based PROLETAR is Black Room's second ever resident and a force to be reckoned with in South Florida's underground. With nearly half a decade of experience playing clubs, bars, warehouses, festivals and everything in between, they boast a sizable resume spanning two continents — with gigs like Space's The Ground and Floyd, iiiPoints, iiiJoints, Fetichista in Romania, and SAGEWERK in Germany.

They've provided support for artists like Henry Brooks, Francois X, Cera Khin, MCMLXXXV, and more.

Resident — 003

Dadrev

Born in Bogotá, Colombia, Dadrev developed a love for music from an early age — especially electronic music. Having been producing for over 8 years, he made the switch to techno after stumbling into an underground rave in Miami. He has shared the stage with Gravedgr, Neon Graveyard, Tanja Miju, Pisapia, Gianni Di Bernardo, Niotech, and many more.

He has played at notable clubs across Miami, New York, Philadelphia, and Tampa, as well as Mexico, Colombia, Chile, and beyond. His music has been supported by Charlie Sparks, Caravel, Vendex, Lee Ann Roberts, and many others.

Specializing in Schranz, Hard Techno, and Hypnotic Groovy Techno, Dadrev makes it his mission to keep the dance floor energetic and leave ravers begging for more.

Resident — 004

DOMNROB

Meet DOMNROB — the dynamic duo from Venezuela now taking the Miami scene by storm. Their sets blend hyper, psy, and industrial sounds into an immersive experience that hits different every time. Beyond the decks, these 24 and 23-year-olds are dedicated event promoters and the founders of Black Room, the techno radio and collective.

They have shared the stage with ALIGNMENT, FATIMA HAJJI, BLK, PISAPIA, KANDER, DON WOEZIK, NEON GRAVEYARD, and many others — building a reputation for infectious energy and pulsating beats that leave crowds craving more.
